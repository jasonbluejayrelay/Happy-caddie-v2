Commands & Help

🎙 Voice Commands

Tap 🎤 Listen for one command, or 🔄 for always-on. In always-on mode, say "golf" first to wake it.

"next shot"Record the shot & start the next
"made it"Hole complete — opens score entry
"driver" · "7 iron" · "pitching wedge"Select that club
"yardage" / "how far"Speak Front / Center / Back distances
"score 5"Set your score for the hole
"birdie" · "par" · "bogey"Set your score by name
"scorecard" · "match"Jump to that tab
"rangefinder"Open the camera rangefinder — aim to lock a target
"range" / "shoot"Read the locked target's distance
"commands" / "help"Show this screen
